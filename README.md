# E-Commerce Customer & Revenue Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist E-commerce Dataset to understand why revenue fluctuates and how customer retention can be improved.

The project includes:
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
- Multi-table Data Merging
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Repeat vs One-Time Customer Analysis
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Payment Method Analysis
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- RFM Segmentation

---

## Business Problem
Why is revenue fluctuating and how can we increase customer retention?

---

## Dataset
Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist (Kaggle)

---

## Tools and Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Analyses

### 1. Monthly Revenue Trend
Analyzed revenue over time to identify growth patterns and seasonality.

### 2. Repeat vs One-Time Customers
Measured customer retention by comparing repeat and one-time purchasers.

### 3. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.

### 4. Payment Method Analysis
Examined which payment methods contributed the most revenue.

### 5. Top Product Categories
Identified the categories generating the highest revenue.

### 6. RFM Segmentation
Segmented customers based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value.

---

## Key Business Insights
- Most customers purchased only once, indicating retention opportunities.
- A small group of customers generated a significant share of revenue.
- Credit card payments accounted for the majority of revenue.
- Top product categories contributed a large portion of total sales.
- RFM analysis identified high-value and loyal customers.
